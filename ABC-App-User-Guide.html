ABC "Beacon" User Guide

The official manual for coordinating and funding public-facing AI alignment research.

⚠️ Requirement: You must have a Web3 wallet (Coinbase Wallet, MetaMask) and some ETH on Base.

1. Core Concepts

What is ABC Beacon?

An on-chain infrastructure to curate and fund AI safety research. It solves the "signal crisis" by paying experts to evaluate new work and creating objective, verifiable reputation scores.

2. Authority Score & Tiers

How It Works

Your Authority Score is a verifiable track record of expertise. It is calculated by:

  • Activity Points: Earned for correct predictions and completed bounties.
  • Conviction Multiplier: 2x bonus for being an "Early Bird" (first 25%).
  • Specialization: 1.5x bonus if >70% of your work is in one domain (e.g., Interpretability).

Tiers

  • Bronze (100-500): Curate papers & view bounties.
  • Silver (500-1k): Propose new bounties (Stake required).
  • Gold (1k-2k): Claim bounties for pay & Verified Curator status.
  • Platinum (2k-3k): Verifier Access (Judge submissions) & Eligible for ASIP Committee.
  • Diamond (3k+): Thought Leader status & Automatic Committee consideration.

3. For Researchers (Bounties)

Grants

Getting Funded

  1. Propose: Users (Silver+) can create a bounty proposal. Staking 500 ABC is required to prevent spam.
  2. Claim: Experts (Gold+) can claim active bounties to work on.
  3. Submit: Upload your evidence (PDF/Code) to IPFS via the "My Bounties" tab.

4. For Verifiers (Governance)

The Verifier Dashboard

Restricted Access: Only Platinum+ members can access the Verifier Dashboard (Password protected).

  • Review: Inspect code and results from submitted bounties.
  • Consensus: 3/5 verifiers must vote "Approve" to release funds.
  • Slashing: Verifiers who vote against the majority maliciously may lose their stake.

5. ASIP Integration

The "Talent Pipeline"

High-performing contributors don't just earn money; they earn influence.

  • Grant Committee: Reaching Platinum Tier makes you eligible to join the ASIP Grant Committee, directing millions in funding.
  • Strategic Prioritization: Aggregate data from ABC (e.g., "High quality but low funding in Agent Foundations") guides ASIP's $50M strategy.

6. Analytics

Strategic Oversight

Use the Analytics tab to view the "Funds Distributed by Category" and "Impact Efficiency" charts, helping the community identify neglected research areas.
