ABC "Beacon" User Guide

The official manual for governing the Aligned Beacon Commons Protocol.

⚠️ Requirement: You must have a Web3 wallet (Coinbase Wallet, MetaMask, or Rainbow) and some ETH on Base to pay for gas fees.

1. Getting Started

Connect Your Wallet

  1. Navigate to the Governance Hub (Home).
  2. Click the button in the top right.
  3. Select your preferred wallet and sign the connection request.
  4. Once connected, you will see your wallet address and balance.

2. For Voters

Governance

Discovering & Voting

Your primary role is to signal conviction on high-impact research proposals.

  • Browse Proposals: Use the "Active Proposals" feed. You can filter by "Hot" or "High Authority".
  • Check Authority: Look for Gold/Platinum badges. These proposers have a proven track record.
  • Vote: Click "Vote with Conviction" on any proposal card. This will prompt a transaction signature to cast your vote on-chain.

3. For Researchers

Grants

Creating a Proposal

Need funding for AI safety research? Apply for a grant.

  1. Click + Create Proposal on the home page.
  2. Details: Enter your title, abstract, and tags. Upload your full PDF/Markdown spec to IPFS.
  3. Logistics: Define your requested budget ($ABC) and timeline.
  4. Stake: You must stake 500 ABC to prevent spam. This is returned if your proposal is valid.

Managing Work

Go to the My Bounties tab to track your active grants and submit evidence (code/papers) for verification.

4. For Verifiers

Reviewing Evidence

Who can verify? While all ABC holders can view evidence, only High-reputation users (Gold Tier+) are selected to vote on fund release to ensure security.

  • Review Code: Inspect the submitted code/PDFs in the "Evidence Panel".
  • Vote: Choose to Approve (release funds) or Reject (if requirements aren't met).
  • Consensus: Funds are only released when a majority of selected verifiers (e.g. 3/5) approve.

5. Analytics

Strategic Oversight

Visit the Analytics tab to see where capital is flowing (e.g., "Interpretability vs. Control") and ensure the ecosystem remains balanced.
